Welcome to my site, my name is Umer. I'm a 27 year old fullstack developer residing in Ottawa, Ontario. My entire life I had been obsessed with technology, always looking forward to what was next. Since I can remember I had a mouse and keyboard in my hand. Originally it was just gaming, but since a young age it had started... I first decided to modify a game to use a different player model, when it changed in front of my eyes, I was hooked. I then shared this with my friends, doing it for them and my reputation as a computer geek began. I always had a passion for this stuff, but growing up I thought I wanted to work in the legal side of things; I pursued criminology at Wilfrid Laurier University, and then completed it at Carleton University. I realized slowly but surely that this wasn't it for me, or not entirely. I have retained and kept all my knowledge from my years in university and hope to apply it in the tech world. I decided to get certified for a hobby that I am now pursuing as a profession. Thank you for visiting my page, and never hesitate to reach out!
